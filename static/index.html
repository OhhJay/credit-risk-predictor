AI-Powered Credit Risk Scoring

Predict whether a borrower will default on their loan — before it happens. Built on the Home Credit Default Risk dataset (307K applicants, 13.6M payment records).

🎯 The Approach

Started with a question: "If I were a credit analyst, what would I want to know?"

👤
Who are they?
Profile, income, employment stability
💳
How do they pay us?
13.6M installment payment records
🏦
How do they pay others?
1.7M external credit bureau records

📊 Feature Engineering (80% of the value)

From 8 raw columns in the installments table, engineered 17 features using a consistent framework:

Level
Average behavior
Volatility
Consistency
Extremes
Worst behavior
Trends
Getting better/worse
Recency
Recent behavior

💡 Key Insight

The min_payment_ratio (worst single payment someone ever made) was far more predictive than any average. Defaulters don't behave badly on average — they behave badly sometimes. Their worst moments betray them.

Good borrowers
min ratio = 1.0
Always paid in full
Defaulters
min ratio = 0.47
Once paid < half
